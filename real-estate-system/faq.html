Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How do I list my property?
Go to "Add Property" from dashboard. Fill all details and submit. Your property will be reviewed and listed.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, mobile money (MTN MoMo, Airtel Money), and bank transfers.
How long does booking confirmation take?
Most bookings are confirmed within 24 hours. You will receive email notification.
Can I cancel my booking?
Yes, you can cancel bookings from "My Bookings" page. Cancellation policy varies by property.
Is my data secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data.
